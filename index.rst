How to Connect Fubo on Your TV ?
================================

You can easily activate Fubo on your TV using ``fubo.tv/connect``.
Just open the Fubo app on your TV, choose **Sign in with a code**, and note the 6-digit code displayed.
Then visit ``fubo.tv/connect`` on your phone or computer, enter the code, and sign in with your Fubo account.
In a few seconds, your TV will be connected and ready to stream your favorite Fubo channels.

Step-by-Step Guide
------------------

1. Turn on your Smart TV or streaming device.
2. Make sure it’s connected to the internet.
3. Go to your TV’s App Store (like Roku, Fire TV, Google Play, or Apple TV).
4. In the search bar, type **Fubo**.
5. Select the Fubo app and click **Install** or **Download**.
6. Once installed, open the Fubo app.
7. On the welcome screen, choose **Sign In**.
8. Select **Sign in with a code** — a 6-digit code will appear on your TV screen.
9. On your phone or computer, open a browser and visit **fubo.tv/connect**.
10. Enter the 6-digit code from your TV.
11. Click **Submit** to continue.
12. Sign in with your Fubo account email and password if asked.
13. Wait a few seconds — your TV screen will refresh and connect automatically.
14. You’re now ready to watch Fubo on your TV!
